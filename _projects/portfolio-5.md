In my [critique] (https://harshmeel.github.io/files/A critique climate change.pdf), I explored two influential documentaries on climate change - An Inconvenient Truth and The Great Global Warming Swindle . The choice of films presents two opposing perspectives : one is alarmist, and the other totally denies the former. My focus is on dissecting their arguments, assessing their scientific accuracy, and highlighting how they are trying to shape public perception.
